Our Journey







Four high-schoolers, one month, one crazy idea. Last summer, our team met at a 1-month entrepreneurship summer camp at MIT called LaunchX. Being campers ourselves, we were fed up with the current campware solutions on the market which were too clunky and too small. And even worse, non-reusable plastic cookware solutions pollute camping sites. We knew we had to do create a product that was convenient and not harmful to the environment. After spending countless hours researching and brainstorming, Falcn was born. Falcn aims to equip campers with a compatible, environmentally friendly and durable cookware solution. We wanted to not only create a convenient product, but one that will solve the bigger problem by eliminating plastic pollution one bag at a time.
